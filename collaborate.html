Who this is for

This page is mainly for fellow students who are working on:

  • course projects
  • research ideas
  • hackathon or side projects
  • data-for-good or public-interest work

I’m also open to thoughtful, small-scope collaborations beyond student spaces when expectations are clear and aligned.

Ways we could collaborate

Project brainstorming & scoping

Turning vague ideas into something buildable.

Data analysis & visualization

Making sense of datasets for projects, competitions, or personal exploration.

ML / AI experiments

Trying models, evaluating results, and documenting what we learn (including what doesn’t work).

Research & writing support

Helping structure ideas, figures, and explanations for papers, reports, or presentations.

What collaboration looks like

When I collaborate, I value:

  • shared learning
  • clear communication
  • small, well-defined scope
  • documenting as we go
  • shared credit

If this sounds like something you’d enjoy, feel free to reach out and tell me what you’re working on and what kind of collaboration you’re hoping for.
